# TADKA Luxe - Premium Design Language

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Identity
TADKA Luxe embodies the fusion of **modern luxury** with **Indian traditional warmth**, creating an immersive dining experience that feels both contemporary and culturally rich. The design language draws inspiration from premium Indian restaurants like Indian Accent, Suvarna Mahal, and Armani/Amal, while maintaining a unique identity that sets new standards in restaurant SaaS platforms.

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Royal Maroon `#3B0A45` - Represents luxury, tradition, and warmth
- **Accent**: Gold Gradient `#FFD700 â†’ #F6C90E` - Symbolizes premium quality and celebration
- **Neutral**: Ivory `#FFF8E7` - Provides elegance and readability
- **Dark**: Charcoal `#1B1B1B` - Creates depth and sophistication

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Playfair Display - Elegant serif for headings and hero text
- **Body Font**: Inter - Clean, modern sans-serif for optimal readability
- **Accent Font**: Cormorant Garamond - For special emphasis and luxury touches

### Visual Language
- **Minimal Luxe Fusion**: Clean lines with ornate Indian motifs
- **Cinematic Depth**: Layered compositions with subtle shadows and highlights
- **Golden Hour Lighting**: Warm, inviting color temperatures throughout
- **Organic Textures**: Subtle spice-inspired patterns and natural elements
